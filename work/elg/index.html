ELG – iOS App

Made for the students, their parents and the teachers of the secondary school “Elisabeth-Gymnasium Halle”, ELG offers easy and fast access to information on absentee covers, the latest news and the schedule.

Since the release of the first version in 2012, I have been developing the app further and optimizing it for the latest software and hardware.
